History of Computers

Charles Babbage invented the ﬁrst mechanical computer. Perhaps, this is why he got the title of "Father of Computers". Modern computers are based on his analytical machine.

Among the early inventors of computers, Lady Ada Lovelace was perhaps one of the most remarkable one. She is often credited to be the ﬁrst ever programmer. Additionally, she was the ﬁrst one to use an algorithm.
